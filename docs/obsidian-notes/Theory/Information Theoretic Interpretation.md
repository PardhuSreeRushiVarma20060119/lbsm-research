# Information-Theoretic Interpretation

The **entropy** feature acts as the primary information-theoretic signal in LBSM.

## Entropy as Behavioral Uncertainty

|Entropy Level|Meaning|
|---|---|
|High|Exploration, instability, behavioral uncertainty|
|Low|Convergence, deterministic execution|

Entropy = policy randomness indicator. A deterministic policy (stable regime) produces low entropy. An exploration policy (exploratory regime) produces maximum entropy.

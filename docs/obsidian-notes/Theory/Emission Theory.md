# Emission Theory

## Core Statement

Observable telemetry is:

> A noisy behavioral projection of hidden latent dynamics.

Telemetry is **not** the behavior itself. It is evidence of hidden behavioral structure.

## Formal Model

$$x_t \sim \mathcal{N}(\mu_{s_t},\ \Sigma_{s_t})$$

Every behavioral regime has its own statistical signature. Telemetry distributions **encode behavioral identity**.

## Why This Matters

The ontological shift: traditional telemetry systems treat observations as raw data. LBSM treats them as projections of a latent process. This changes what you do with the data — you don't analyze the values, you analyze the geometry of the projection.

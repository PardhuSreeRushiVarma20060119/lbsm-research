# Regime Boundary Theory

## Statement

Behavioral transitions **do not occur randomly** across the manifold. They localize near manifold boundaries, bridge regions, and geometric bottlenecks.

## Evidence

`transition_coords.csv` contains 14,161 regime-change events with UMAP coordinates. KDE of these coordinates peaks in the bridge region between stable–adaptive–exploratory.

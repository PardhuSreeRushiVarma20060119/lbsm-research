# Stability Theory

Stable regimes exhibit: low entropy, low velocity, low manifold dispersion, high local density. Stable behavior forms **compact attractors** in UMAP space.

|Property|Stable Value|
|---|---|
|Entropy|Low|
|Velocity|Low|
|Manifold dispersion|Low|
|Local density|High|

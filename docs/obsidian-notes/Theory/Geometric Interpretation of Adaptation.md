# Geometric Interpretation of Adaptation

## Statement

Adaptation = **smooth trajectory traversal between behavioral attractor regions**.

Not discrete state-switching. Not an instantaneous transition. A continuous, measurable geometric path.

## Significance

This reframes "learning" or "adaptation" as a geometric quantity:

- Measurable via path length in the bridge region
- Has a direction (toward stable)
- Has a speed (manifold velocity during adaptation)
- Has efficiency (tortuosity of adaptive segments)

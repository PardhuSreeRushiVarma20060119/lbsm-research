# Instability Theory

Unstable regimes exhibit: high entropy, high variance, high velocity, high tortuosity, weak attractor structure. Unstable behavior forms **diffuse manifold regions**.

|Property|Unstable Value|
|---|---|
|Entropy|High|
|Velocity|High|
|Manifold dispersion|High|
|Tortuosity|High|
|Attractor structure|Diffuse / absent|
